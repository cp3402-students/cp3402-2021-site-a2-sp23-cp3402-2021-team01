Laughton Leadlight is a small business offering one of a kind handcrafted leadlight home décor. All pieces are designed and crafted in a small studio in Brisbane by Diane Laughton-Holland.


Currently, Laughton Leadlight offer a range of home décor including suncatchers, planter stakes and terrariums, as well as custom pieces within the same range.

The future will still offer this range but will focus on leadlight house number signs, carriage lanterns and candle holders, and will also offer workshops.
