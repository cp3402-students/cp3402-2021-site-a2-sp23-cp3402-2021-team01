Shipping & Returns Policy

Shipping policy

What is the cost of shipping?

Shipping is free for all orders above 50AUD. All orders below 50AUD will have a shipping charge of 15AUD

How long does shipping usually take

From the time of order placed, it takes about 6-7 days for domestic orders and 8-10 days for international orders to reach you.

If you have placed an order with multiple items, your items may arrive in multiple shipments. The estimated delivery times are indicative, and, on some occasions, there might be some unavoidable delays beyond our control. We will keep you informed in case of any delays.

How can I track my order

For domestic orders, once your order has been dispatched, you can track the order using the reference number provided by our logistics partner

You can track the status of your package 24 hours after your order is dispatched from our warehouse or vendor location

What can I do in-case of delays

We always strive to deliver the products within the specified time frame. In the unlikely event that happens, you can contact us on support@leadlights.com

Returns, Refunds, Cancellations and Exchanges

What is the return policy at LeadLights

Most of our products are eligible for return and replacements for domestic orders. There are some items that are Made to order which are not eligible for returns. Please note that international orders are not eligible for returns or exchanges.

All products are only valid for return for 30 days from the date of delivery
What is the return process

Once you request to return a product, a pick-up is organised for the item. Our logistics partners will come to pick up the item within 3-5 business days after your return request has been received. Once pickup is done, your refund will be initiated from our end.

Cancellation of order

You can cancel your order within 24 hours of placing the order

Refund process

In case of prepaid orders, money will be returned to the bank account/credit/debit card where the payment was made from. For Cash on Delivery orders customers will be required to provide bank account details where they would like to receive the refund.

How long does the refund process take

The refund will reflect in your bank account within seven business days in case of cancellation of an order. In case of returns, we will start the refund process after the product has been picked up by our courier partner and will take 7 business days after that to reflect in your account
