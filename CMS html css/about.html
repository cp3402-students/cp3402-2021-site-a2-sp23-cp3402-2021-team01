About Laughton Leadlight

After learning the art of leadlight and copper foiling from a number of teachers in both Toowoomba and Brisbane, Diane Laughton-Holland from Laughton Leadlight has been handcrafting glass art since 2015 from a small studio in Kedron. Diane enjoys working with her clients to create house number signs that feel truly one of a kind and enhance the look and feel of your home.
